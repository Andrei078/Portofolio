// COMMANDS

What AkibaBot Can Do

General

General Commands

Utility and fun commands available to all members. Check profiles, challenge friends, or just have fun with classic mini-games.

+avatar Display a user's avatar
+profile View your server profile & stats
+rps Play rock paper scissors vs the bot
+tictactoe Challenge a member to tic-tac-toe
+serverinfo Show server details & statistics
+afk Set an AFK status with a message
+ping Check the bot's response latency
+banner Display a user's profile banner
Economy

Economy Commands

A full virtual economy system. Earn coins, gamble at the slots, manage your bank, shop for items, and trade with other members.

+daily Claim your daily coin reward
+slot Spin the slot machine and win big
+roulette Bet your coins on roulette
+bank Check your wallet & bank balance
+deposit / +withdraw Move coins between wallet and bank
+shop Browse & purchase available items
+inventory View, equip & unequip your items
+give Transfer coins to another member
+top Display the richest members leaderboard
Admin

Admin Commands

Full moderation toolkit for server staff. Manage members, control roles, and run giveaways — all with fine-grained permission control.

+ban / +unban Ban or unban a member from the server
+mute / +unmute Silence or restore a member's voice
+createrole Create a new role with custom settings
+addrole Assign a role to a specific member
+removerole Remove a role from a member
+giveaway start Launch a new giveaway with a timer
+giveaway end End a running giveaway early
+giveaway reroll Pick a new winner for a past giveaway
